The org chart is the authority graph

A founder grants a CEO agent a weekly budget; the CEO re-delegates thinner slices down the chain. Every seat’s spending power is a delegation no agent can exceed, and re-delegation can only ever attenuate. Caps are cryptographic, enforced offline at the ad platform — a faithful replay of go run ./examples/charter.
